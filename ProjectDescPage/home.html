Exploring the Use of Prediction Markets

As Risk Assessment and Hedging Tools in the Financial Industry

Explore Framework Try It Out

Project Scope

A comprehensive exploration of theoretical and practical dimensions to demonstrate how real-time probability estimates from blockchain-based prediction markets offer a more transparent, efficient, and dynamic alternative to traditional derivatives for hedging tail risks and identifying new opportunities.

03

Institutional Applications

Parametric Insurance and Commodity Hedging with Probabilistic Triggers

Exploring the adaptation of prediction markets for institutional risk management, focusing on parametric insurance models that use automated triggers based on probabilistic thresholds derived from crowd-sourced market consensus and commodity hedging strategies informed by real-time prediction market probabilities. It evaluates through case studies, assessing integration feasibility, regulatory compliance, and risk reduction benefits, while also covering practical platform development activities.

Data Protocols for Contract Settlement

Accurate Mapping of Real-World Outcomes to On-Chain Contracts

Addressing the integration of decentralized oracles and data-extraction protocols to securely retrieve, validate, and map real-world outcomes — such as election results or economic indicators — to on-chain binary prediction market contracts, thereby enhancing contract reliability, reducing resolution disputes, and building institutional trust.
